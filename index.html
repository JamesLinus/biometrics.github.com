OpenBR -- Open Source Biometric Recognition

Open Algorithms and Reproducible Evaluations

MITRE

OpenBR is a collaborative research project of The MITRE Corporation. The MITRE Corporation is a not-for-profit organization chartered to work in the public interest. As a national resource, we apply our expertise in systems engineering, information technology, operational concepts, and enterprise modernization to address our sponsors' critical needs.

Development

Supporting the development and evaluation of biometric algorithms in an accessible, reproducible, and transparent manner. OpenBR is licensed under Apache 2.0 and follows the Semantic Versioning convention for publishing releases. The project uses the CMake build system and depends on Qt 4.8 and OpenCV 2.4.3.
