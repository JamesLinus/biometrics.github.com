Apply the transform, taking the full template list as input. A TemplateList is what is typically passed from transform to transform. Transforms that just need to operatoe on a single template at a time (and want to output exactly 1 template) can implement project(template), but transforms that want to change the structure of the TemplateList (such as flatten), or or output more or less than one template (e.g. detection methods) should implement project(TemplateList) directly.
